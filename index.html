Shelter makes sure that your safety is under your control. We have done our best to simplify each and every step in providing you with the utmost safety in any situation.

How It Works

Add Your Contacts

Add your contacts so they'll be notified when you are in danger.

Turn “ON” the protection

Turn "ON" the protection. Once protection is turned "ON", the app starts listening to you.

Shout “Help”

When the app recognizes the word "Help", the SOS will be sent to the contacts you have added.

You’re safe :)

The SOS consists of a text message along with a link to your LIVE location (depends whether you've access to internet) This live location will continue to track you until the protection is turned "OFF"
